About us

Make it easy, Make it instant

EC food products is one of the major food processing companies in Kerala. We have been serving instant foods in kerala for the past 12 years. We started our journey with half cooked chapathi production and now it has expanded to more instant products.Our ingenuity lies in the quality of our products. Our superior services, expertise and devotion to customer satisfaction make us a first class business establishment. With commitment to serve with exellence and candor, our professional merit is un paralled in the field. We depend fully on our impecable quality tied together with highest of standards and reliable services as our medium of advertising.

Ec food products are prepared by experts with perfect precautions and preservation techniques. Our mission is to serve food for people who are not into cooking. Our products are inevitable need for people with extraordinary goals and least time. All they need to do is heat it up.
